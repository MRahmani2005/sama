Sama Xan Biography

Biography

Sama Khodai, known by her stage name Sama Xan, was born on June 21, 2000. She is currently years old. Sama Xan was born and lives in the beautiful city of Bokan. She began her professional career in Kurdish music at the age of 24. Most of her artistic presence and activities so far have been on Instagram and TikTok platforms, and her main style is inspired by Kurdish folk music. Her first official release, titled Azar, was published on May 19, 2024; a track that marks the beginning of her professional journey in Kurdish music. The lyrics and melody of this piece were entirely written and composed by Sama Xan herself. Her second release, named Nafarat, was published on April 30, 2025. Her third track, titled Gasht, was released on May 13, 2025. Sama Xan quickly became one of the most popular and beloved figures in virtual music spaces. Her emotional voice, sincere performances, and unique artistic personality earned her the title of “The Trendy Sweet-Voiced Girl of Instagram” among fans. With high dedication, she is now on the path of producing more works, creating artistic videos, and interacting with her audience. It seems that a bright future awaits her in the world of Kurdish music and beyond.
